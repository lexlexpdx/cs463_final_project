About Me

Hello! My name is Lex (he/him), and I am a Senior majoring in Computer Science at Portland State University. I decided to make a switch from a profession in healthcare to one in Computer Science around two years ago

I am most interested in leveraging machine learning and deep learning to improve medical imaging analysis and subsequently improve patient outcomes.

When I'm not working on homework for classes I enjoy spending my time with my wife, our three rats, and our cat. I also have a deep love for pinball, video games, and reading.
